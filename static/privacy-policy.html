Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 8, 2026

CardChecker ("we", "our", or "the App") is a mobile application that allows users to scan Pokémon trading cards using their device camera and retrieve market pricing information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Camera images. When you use the scan feature, your device camera captures a photo of a trading card. This image is sent to our server for recognition and is not stored after processing is complete. Images are discarded immediately after generating results.

Device information. We may automatically collect basic technical data such as device type, operating system version, app version, and screen resolution. This is used solely for crash reporting and improving compatibility.

Usage analytics. We collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., number of scans, feature usage) to improve the App. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

Purchase data. If you subscribe to a premium plan, payment is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment card details, billing address, or other financial information. We only receive a purchase confirmation token from Google Play.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not require account registration.
  • We do not access your contacts, location, microphone, or files beyond the camera.
  • We do not use cookies or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell, share, or rent any data to third parties.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Card recognition: Camera images are processed by our AI model to identify the card and return pricing data.
  • Service improvement: Anonymous usage statistics help us improve accuracy and add new features.
  • Crash diagnostics: Device information helps us fix bugs and ensure compatibility.

4. Data Retention

Card images are processed in real-time and deleted immediately after the response is sent. We do not maintain a database of user-submitted images. Anonymous usage statistics are retained for up to 12 months.

5. Data Security

All communication between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). Our servers are hosted in the European Union (Hetzner Cloud, Germany) and are protected by standard security measures including firewalls, access controls, and regular updates.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may display links to external marketplaces (CardMarket, TCGPlayer, PriceCharting, eBay) for price information. When you follow these links, you leave our App and are subject to those websites' privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

We use Google Gemini AI for optional card recognition features. When Gemini is used, the card image is sent to Google's API. Google's privacy policy applies to this processing. No personal user data is sent to Google — only the card image.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is rated for general audiences and contains no user-generated content or social features.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data or require accounts, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. If you have concerns about data processing, you can contact us at the address below.

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) have rights under GDPR including the right to access, rectification, erasure, and data portability. Since we do not store personal data, these rights are satisfied by design.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
